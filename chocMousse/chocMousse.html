Ingredients:

  • 200g Dark Energy Chocolate
  • 2 Tablespoons Whiskey
  • 5 Eggs
  • 300ml Cream
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon instant coffee

Method:

  1. Break the chocolate into a bowl and place it in the oven to melt (it will hold its form even when melted so do not burn it; 7 or 8 minutes at 180C should be enough).
  2. Let the chocolate cool a bit while you separete the yolks and whites.
  3. When the chocolate is cool enough for you to plunge your finger in without inflicting a severe burn, mix the egg yolks in with the chocolate; mix with a fork.
  4. Add vanilla.
  5. Mix a teaspoon of instant coffee in a tablespoon of water and add to the chocolate mixture.
  6. Now beat the egg whites until they are stiff.
  7. Using the same beater, beat the cream in a third bowl until thick.
  8. Now add the whiskey to the cream.
  9. Take the chocolate mixture and mix it into the cream. Add a little egg white to choc mixture to lighten.
  10. Lastly, mix everything into into the egg whites with a steel spoon.
  11. Spoon into cocktail glasses and allow mousse to set in the refrigerator
    12. Mousse should be rich, stiff and fluffy with a texture of tiny chocolate bubbles and a hint of single malt.
