www.OneFüd.tk Chocolate-covered Strawberry Milkshake

Ingredients:

  • ⅔ cup fat-free milk
  • ⅔ cup reduced-fat strawberry ice cream
  • ⅔ cup frozen unsweetened sliced strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon fat-free hot fuddge ice cream topping
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream in a can
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Method:

  1. Combine the milk, ice cream and strawberries in a blender; cover and process until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the inside of a chilled glass with fudge topping
  3. Add ice cream mixture
  4. Garnish with whipped cream and a cherry on top
  5. Eat now young one, you deserve it
