Ingredients

Makes approximately 30 Fritters but size of fritters could make more or less fritters.
  • 1 cup Self Raising Flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Half a cup of grated cheese
  • 1 strip of chopped spring onion
  • 2 slices of finely chopped ham
  • Half a can of corn kearnels
  • *Optional* Add water from Corn Kearnels for easier mixing!

Method

  1. Gradually mix the milk with the Self Raising flour in a mixing bowl than stir in the egg.
  2. Stir in all the remaining ingredients into the mixing bowl
  3. Heat oil in frying pan at medium heat and drop spoonfuls of the batter into pan
  4. Cook until bubbles appear on the uncooked side than turn over or until the side being cooked is a golden brown.
  5. Allow fritters to cool on plate with paper towling
  6. Serve with salt, pepper, tomato sauce or even pickles!!!!. Enjoy!

      7. Allergen information: Contains Egg and Milk

      These fried fritters are perfect as an entree
