www.OneFüd.tk Lemon and Lime Bitters

Ingredients:

  • 1.25 litres lemonade, chilled

  • 1/2 cup lime juice cordial

  • 1 teaspoon Angostura Bitters (see note)

  • 1 cup ice cubes

  • Angostura Bitters and lemon slices, to serve

Method:

  1. Combine lemonade, cordial and bitters in a jug. Divide ice between 4 glasses. Shake a few drops of bitters into each glass. Top with lemonade mixture. Serve with lemon slices.
