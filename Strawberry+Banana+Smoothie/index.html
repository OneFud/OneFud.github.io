www.OneFüd.tk Strawberry & Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 x 250g punnet strawberries, hulled, washed.

  • 2 overripe bananas, coarsely chopped.

  • 750ml (3 cups) milk.

  • 260g (1 cup) vanilla-falvoured yoghurt.

Method:

  1. Place half the strawberries, banana, milk and yoghurt in the jug of a blender and blend until smooth. Pour evenly among 3 serving glasses.

  2. Repeat with the remaining strawberries, banana, milk and yoghurt. Serve immediately.
