www.Füd.com.au Coco Pops Ice Cream Cone

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 bag of Marshmallows
  • 1 cup of Coco Pops
  • 200g of Butter
  • Ice cream (flavour optional)

METHOD


1.Heat up a fry pan and melt the butter

2.Put in the marshmellows in the fry pan and melt them

3.Turn off the heat and put the coco pops in the melted marshmellows

4.Mix the marshmellows and the coco pops together

5.Lay the mix out on baking paper on a tray and put in a freezer for 15mins

6.Remove from freezer and cut out pizza slice shapes and wrap them into a cone

7.Place into a glass and freezer for another 15mins

8.Put in ice cream and enjoy!!
