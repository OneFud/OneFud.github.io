Ingredients:

  • 12 thick-sliced bacon strips
  • 12 wooden skewers(approxiamtely 30cm)
  • 170 grams of white candy coating, chopped
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Method:

  1. Thread each strip of bacon onto a wooden skewer. Place on a rack in a large baking pan. Bake at 400 degrees celsius for 20-25 minutes or until crisp. Cool completely.
  2. In a microwave, melt candy coating; stir until smooth. Combine chocolate chips and shortening; melt in a microwave and stir until smooth.
  3. With pastry brushes, coat bacon on both sides with melted coatings. Top each strip as desired. Place on waxed paper-lined baking sheets. Refrigerate until firm. Store in the refrigerator.
  4. Serve with any of your favourite sweet toppings or even pickles for a healthier topping! Enjoy!
